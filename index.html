Hey there! I'm Joshua Prado, a photographer based in Madison, Wisconsin. I've been taking photos for over four years with experience in Student Newspapers, Professional Headshots, Senior Portraits, Engagements, Drone Photography and Photojournalism. Currently, I work as an Assistant Photographer for the University of Wisconsin - Madison School of Business. I also manage social media accounts for Greek InterVarsity and my fraternity, Phi Kappa Theta. I enjoy capturing the hidden moments that occur in everyday life, and creating memorable portraits and memories to cherish for years to come. My photography Instagram can be found at @jpxphoto
